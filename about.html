The story behind Manevi Rota

What is Manevi Rota?

Manevi Rota is an AI-powered web application that helps users discover meaningful one-day routes across Turkey, combining spiritual landmarks and natural wonders.

How does it work?

Select a city and your preferred route type. Our AI engine generates a personalized itinerary featuring mosques, historical sites, forests, and nature trails — all in one day.

Technologies Used

  • HTML5 & CSS3 — structure and styling
  • JavaScript (ES6) — dynamic features
  • OpenRouter API — AI route generation
  • LocalStorage — saving your favorite routes

Why Manevi Rota?

Turkey is home to thousands of years of spiritual heritage and breathtaking nature. Manevi Rota was built to make these experiences accessible to everyone — one route at a time.
